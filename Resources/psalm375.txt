PSALM 37:5
May is about to end, and June is about to start. Let this be our prayer:

“Dear God, I don’t know where You are taking me this June… but I trust You.”

I don’t know what this month will bring.
The opportunities, the challenges, the waiting, or the unexpected moments in between.

But before anything even happens, I choose to place it all in Your hands.

Lead me where You want me to go.
Protect me from what is not meant for me.
And help me trust You even when the path feels unclear.

Because I know that Your plans are always greater than my understanding.

“Commit your way to the Lord; trust in Him…” Psalm 37:5

So whether this month brings blessings, lessons, delays, or breakthroughs, let me walk through it with faith instead of fear.

Lord, go before me this June. Guide my heart, order my steps, and remind me that wherever You lead me is exactly where I need to be. 🙇🏻🤍
