ISAIAH 60:22
“Nasa proseso ka pa…”

Kaya huwag kang magmadali.

Be patient with yourself—good things take time, and growth takes time.

Masakit sa simula, pero unti-unti, kakayanin mo rin.

Rejection, disappointment, pain; kasama lahat ‘yan sa proseso, pero hindi ibig sabihin na hindi ka makakarating.

Hinahanda ka pa Niya—He’s working behind the scenes, even when you can’t see it.

Darating din ang araw na ngingitian mo na lang ang season na ‘to.

Keep showing up. Take it one day at a time.

You will get there, in God’s perfect time 🙇🏻☝🏻
