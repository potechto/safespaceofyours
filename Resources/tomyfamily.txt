Happy Mother's Day
Minsan nakakalimutan ko na tumatanda na pala sila.
Sa isip ko, sila pa rin yung malalakas, yung laging nandiyan, yung hindi napapagod.
Pero habang tumatagal, napapansin ko na yung maliliit na pagbabago,
yung mas mabagal na galaw, yung pagod sa boses, yung katahimikan na dati wala naman.

I get busy with my own life. School, friends, problems, lahat parang urgent.
And sometimes, hindi ko namamalayan na napapasagot ako ng maiksi, napapabayaan ko silang kausapin, or
I take their presence for granted. But the truth is, time isn't kind to them the way I assume it is.
Habang ako ay tumatanda, sila naman ay unti-unting napapagod.

They don't always ask for much. Minsan simpleng kwentuhan lang, simpleng pag-upo sa tabi nila, simpleng "kumusta ka?" is enough.
Pero kahit gano'n kasimple, nakakalimutan ko pa rin ibigay.
And that's what hurts the most, realizing na habang may oras pa,
hindi ko pa lubos napaparamdam kung gaano ko sila na-aappreciate.
So now, I'll try. I'll try to be more patient, more present, more kind, more better.

Kasi darating ang araw na hahanap-hanapin ko yung mga simpleng moments na meron pa ako ngayon.
And I don't want to regret the times I could have been softer, kinder, and more loving, habang nandito pa sila.
Nay, Tay, to my family, Sorry for the moments I've wasted for someone, for others
Mga oras, mga sandali na dapat hindi pala sakanila kundi para sainyo

Mga bagay na hindi para sakanila kundi para sainyo
Pagod at pagmamahal na dapat isinukli ko sa bawat sakripisyo
Pagod at Pagmamahal na hindi pala dapat para lang kanino
I've wasted so much time for them that I realized I should've spent more on you, on us

And I am so thankful kay Lord that He gave me the chance to realize all of this.
Matagal na pala akong nawawala dati, kaya may inalis siya sa akin.
Hindi niya pala inalis para lalo akong mawala, inalis niya pala para matutunan kong makabalik.

Hindi ako nagsisisi sa naligaw ako, at natuon sa isang bagay palayo sa inyo,
dahil noong natuto akong alisin niya ito, mas lalo akong napalapit pabalik sa totoong ako.
I was lost before when I had one, yet when that one went away, I was found.
Before, I thought I was a better version of myself, but now I know I can be the best.

You asked me before, why not find someone again, why not try to love again
Tapos sagot ko sainyo, ayaw ko muna, nakakatakot palang mag mahal ng sobra sa iba
But the real thing is, I am never scared of loving someone again pala, I am more scared of losing myself again
I am more scared of pleasing them to the moment na makakalimutan ko nanaman na I have you, I have a family

Hindi naman pala nakakatakot mag mahal, nakakatakot lang pala mawala ulit
And maybe, that's the real truth behind your question "why not try to love again"
Because I am scared to lose my track with you
I am scared to lose myself again knowing that I am slowly finding every piece I have missed.....
