Have you ever noticed? Every time we say, “God will provide,” He always shows up in ways we didn’t expect.

And when we look back, we see it was never by accident. It always came at the right time, even if we didn’t understand it then.

Sometimes we pray for one thing, but God gives something different. At first, it can feel like delay or change of plans. But later on, we realize it was actually for our good.

It may not always be what we asked for, but it is always what we needed. God sees what we cannot see, and He knows what is best for us.

So even when things feel unsure, we can still trust Him. He never forgets, never fails, and never comes too late.

“The Lord will provide.” — Genesis 22:14 🙇🏻🤍
